Tips

      Match to Win, Match to Tie, Mis-match to Lose!

To win, create a formation of 2 balls of the same color in a row.

To tie, match 2 balls of the same color, forcing a draw and a new turn.

But beware, if you play a ball with a color that doesn't match the existing formation, you'll lose your turn and give your opponent a chance to win!

Remember, the goal is to create a colorful connection and outmatch your opponent. Will you emerge victorious?
